[+] Normalization & The 0-1 Philosophy

In this theory, 0 represents a state where potential exists but motion has not yet begun—a state of perfect balance. 1 represents the "Limit" where energy becomes dominant and starts its free movement.

If the largest forces in the universe cannot surpass the limit of 1, then perhaps from a philosophical standpoint, we are all part of a single, unified system where no single force can truly dominate the whole.

[+] Dark Matter & Compression Theory

In my view, tension is a much stronger source of energy than gravity; otherwise, the universe couldn't keep expanding.

If we ever exit the reach of Dark Matter or if Dark Energy runs out, it could be the end of everything—a kind of 'doomsday scenario.'
