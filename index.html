[+] Core Concept: The Balloon Analogy

When you apply pressure to a balloon, the reason the surface stretches intensely and the structure collapses inward is that Gravity adjusts that Tension and traps it within the largest possible area.

X = Tension: The resistance force acting upon the surface of the system.

[+] Definition of Variables

  • m (Mass): The fundamental substance of the system.
  • c² (Speed of Light Squared): Directional and spherical expansion of energy.
  • B (v/c): The ratio of velocity to the speed of light.
  • g (Gravity): The pull at the center of the substance.

[+] Calculation Methodology

Multiply the mass by the square of light. Find the ratio of velocity to light (B) and integrate its power into the process. Divide the resulting value by the gravitational force (10⁻ᵍ).
